# Website Structure – Our First Steps (OFST)
**Shopify Tinker Theme** (Native sections + Liquid compatible)

## Brand Personality
Humble, honest, and warmly human — like a fellow exhausted parent sitting next to you on the playground, sharing what actually worked today while admitting what didn’t. Real first steps together, no judgment, just hustle side-by-side.

## Color Palette (from Logo)
- **Background / Base**: `#E4DDD3` — soft, calming main backgrounds and cards  
- **Primary / Logo**: `#7EC8D8` — buttons, links, navigation highlights, trust  
- **Text / Headers**: `#264653` — body text, headings, dark panels  
- **CTA / Energetic Accent**: `#FF6A6A` — call-to-action buttons, highlights  
- **Playful Secondary Accent**: `#FFCC5C` — fun details and warm touches  

All combinations must meet WCAG AA contrast. Prioritize mobile readability.

## Typography
Use **Inter** (system sans-serif fallback).  
- H1: 48px / 36px (700)  
- H2: 32px / 28px (600)  
- Body: 16–17px (400), line height 1.65  
- Buttons & UI: 14–16px (500–600)  

Bold weights for headings/buttons. Keep body text easy to read one-handed.

## Logo & Photography Rules
- Follow logo placement, clear space, and variants exactly (full color on light backgrounds, white on dark).  
- Imagery: Warm, real-life messy joy — candid parent + child moments, hands-on play with magnetic wooden STEM toys, natural lighting, rule of thirds, focus on interaction and connection. Avoid stiff stock photos.

## Simplified Website Structure

### Header Navigation (Tinker Mega Menu)
- **Logo** (left, sticky header)
- **Shop** (mega menu – 3–4 columns)
  - Wooden STEM Toys (magnetic learning toys)
  - Phone Book Covers
  - Kids Clothing
  - All Products / Sale
- **Activities Together** (blog + content hub)
- **Free Resources** (guides, downloads)
- **Our Story**
- Account / Cart icons (right)

### Homepage (Content-First + Shop Flow – Mobile-Friendly)
Use Tinker’s native sections and blocks for easy editing. Keep it calm and scannable for tired parents.

1. **Hero Banner** (full-width, soft `#E4DDD3` base + `#7EC8D8` accent)  
   Warm welcome image (parent + child playing), big humble headline, primary CTA button (“Shop Our Magnetic Toys” or “Start Exploring Together”).

2. **Featured Wooden STEM Toys** (collection grid or bento-style cards)  
   Highlight magnetic wooden learning toys as the star products. Show real play moments. Strong “Shop Now” CTAs.

3. **Content Hub Teaser – Activities Together**  
   Video section or carousel: “See Our Videos”, “Bedtime Stories”, “Parent Guides”, “Play Ideas”.  
   Mix short videos, rich text, and image + text blocks. Encourage parents to explore helpful content.

4. **Shop by Category** (clean collection cards)  
   - Magnetic Wooden STEM Toys  
   - Phone Book Covers  
   - Kids Clothing  

5. **Real Parent Moments** (optional short testimonials or stories – keep light and honest)

6. **Phone Book Cover Banner** (lower on page)  
   Dedicated horizontal or full-width banner showcasing the unique phone book cover product with a gentle CTA (“Discover This Special Parent Tool”).

7. **Newsletter Signup** (prominent, warm section)  
   Headline: “Join the Journey”  
   Description: “Weekly meal ideas, new toy releases, play guides, and occasional discount codes delivered to your inbox.”  
   Simple form with `#7EC8D8` or `#FF6A6A` accent button.

8. **Final Gentle CTA** (optional rich text or image block)  
   “We’re taking these first steps together — one real moment at a time.”

### Core Pages
- **Shop** (main collections page with filters)
- **Collections**:
  - Wooden STEM Toys (magnetic learning toys – main focus)
  - Phone Book Covers

### Footer (Tinker Blocks)
- Newsletter signup (repeat for convenience)
- Quick Links: Shop, Activities Together, Free Resources, Our Story, Policies
- Social Icons
- Copyright + warm tagline: “Real First Steps. No Judgment. Just Hustle Together.”

## Additional Notes for Tinker Theme
- Use native Tinker sections: Hero, Collection List, Video, Rich Text, Image with Text, Newsletter, etc.
- Mega menu for Shop category is fully supported.
- Add custom Liquid sections only where needed (e.g., dynamic content grid for Resources or latest Activities).
- Mobile-first: Large touch targets, generous spacing, easy scrolling.
- Every page/section should feel helpful and end with a gentle CTA to shop or explore content.

This structure makes the site feel more like a **helpful content hub for parents** (videos, stories, guides) while still clearly showcasing and selling the **magnetic wooden STEM toys** as the hero products, with the phone book cover as a unique supporting item lower down.
